# Q1 Marketing Campaign Plan

## Objective

Launch comprehensive marketing campaign for Q1 product release, achieving 10,000 pre-orders and generating 50,000 website visits in the first month.

## Success Criteria

- 10,000 pre-orders secured before launch date
- 50,000 unique website visitors in first month
- 5,000 email subscribers added to list
- 100+ press mentions and reviews
- Social media reach of 500,000+ impressions

**Budget**: $50,000
- Paid advertising: $20,000
- Creative production: $10,000
- Influencer partnerships: $15,000
- Tools and software: $5,000

**Team**:
- 2 Marketing managers
- 1 Designer
- 1 Copywriter
- 1 Social media coordinator

**Timeline**: 4 weeks (Feb 19 - Mar 19, 2026)

## Key Tasks

1. Campaign strategy and planning (Week 1)
2. Creative asset development (Week 1-2)
3. Landing page development (Week 2)
4. Email campaign setup (Week 2)
5. Social media campaign (Week 2-4)
6. PR and media outreach (Week 2-4)
7. Paid advertising launch (Week 3)
8. Launch day execution (Week 4)
9. Post-launch analysis (Week 4)

## Risks and Mitigation

**Risks**:
- Tight timeline may require overtime
- Budget is at upper limit of approval threshold
- Influencer availability uncertain
- Ad platform changes could affect performance

**Mitigation**:
- Buffer time built into schedule
- Backup influencers identified
- Multiple ad platforms for redundancy
- Daily monitoring and adjustment plan
