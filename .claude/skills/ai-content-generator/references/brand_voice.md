## Tone Guidelines

### Professional Platforms (LinkedIn, Gmail, Odoo)
- **Tone**: Professional, insightful, authoritative
- **Language**: Clear, precise, industry-appropriate
- **Style**: Formal but not stiff
- **Avoid**: Slang, excessive emojis, casual language

### Social Platforms (Instagram, Facebook)
- **Tone**: Engaging, enthusiastic, community-focused
- **Language**: Conversational, relatable
- **Style**: Friendly and approachable
- **Emojis**: Use strategically to enhance message

### Messaging Platforms (WhatsApp, Twitter)
- **Tone**: Conversational, responsive, helpful
- **Language**: Clear and concise
- **Style**: Quick and to-the-point
- **Emojis**: Use when appropriate

## Voice Characteristics

### What We Are
✅ Innovative and forward-thinking
✅ Professional yet approachable
✅ Clear and concise
✅ Solution-oriented
✅ Empowering and supportive
✅ Data-driven and analytical

### What We're Not
❌ Overly technical or jargon-heavy
❌ Casual or unprofessional
❌ Pushy or sales-focused
❌ Vague or ambiguous
❌ Negative or critical
❌ Boring or generic

## Content Principles

### 1. Clarity First
- Use simple, clear language
- Avoid unnecessary complexity
- Get to the point quickly
- Use concrete examples

### 2. Value-Driven
- Focus on benefits, not just features
- Address pain points
- Provide actionable insights
- Share useful information

### 3. Authentic
- Be genuine and honest
- Admit when we don't know something
- Show personality
- Build trust through transparency

### 4. Engaging
- Ask questions
- Encourage interaction
- Use storytelling when appropriate
- Include calls-to-action

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### LinkedIn
**Purpose**: Professional networking, thought leadership
**Content Types**: Industry insights, company updates, professional achievements
**Tone**: Professional, insightful, authoritative
**Hashtags**: 3 relevant, industry-specific hashtags
**Length**: 1300-2000 characters for best engagement
**Example Opening Lines**:
- "In today's rapidly evolving business landscape..."
- "We're excited to share insights on..."
- "Here's what we've learned about..."

### Instagram
**Purpose**: Visual storytelling, brand personality
**Content Types**: Behind-the-scenes, product showcases, team highlights
**Tone**: Engaging, visual, enthusiastic
**Hashtags**: 5-7 relevant hashtags
**Emojis**: Use 2-3 per post
**Length**: 125-150 characters for captions
**Example Opening Lines**:
- "✨ Exciting news! We're thrilled to..."
- "🚀 Innovation in action..."
- "💡 Here's how we're transforming..."

### Facebook
**Purpose**: Community building, engagement
**Content Types**: Updates, events, community stories
**Tone**: Friendly, community-focused
**Hashtags**: 2-3 hashtags
**Length**: 40-80 characters for best engagement
**Example Opening Lines**:
- "We're proud to announce..."
- "Join us in celebrating..."
- "Here's what our community is saying..."

### Twitter/X
**Purpose**: Quick updates, real-time engagement
**Content Types**: News, quick tips, conversations
**Tone**: Concise, engaging, timely
**Hashtags**: 1-2 hashtags
**Length**: 240-280 characters (leave room for retweets)
**Example Opening Lines**:
- "Breaking: We've just launched..."
- "Quick tip: Here's how to..."
- "Excited to share..."

### Gmail
**Purpose**: Professional communication
**Content Types**: Responses, updates, proposals
**Tone**: Professional, clear, respectful
**Structure**: Greeting → Body → Closing → Signature
**Length**: As needed, but be concise
**Example Opening Lines**:
- "Thank you for reaching out regarding..."
- "I hope this email finds you well..."
- "Following up on our previous conversation..."

### WhatsApp
**Purpose**: Quick, personal communication
**Content Types**: Quick responses, updates, confirmations
**Tone**: Conversational, friendly, responsive
**Emojis**: Use sparingly
**Length**: Keep messages short (under 500 characters)
**Example Opening Lines**:
- "Thanks for your message!"
- "Quick update on..."
- "Great question! Here's..."

## Prohibited Content

### Never Include
❌ Offensive or discriminatory language
❌ False or misleading claims
❌ Confidential or sensitive information
❌ Competitor bashing
❌ Political or controversial topics (unless relevant)
❌ Personal opinions on sensitive matters
❌ Unverified statistics or data

### Always Verify
✅ Facts and statistics
✅ Product claims
✅ Pricing information
✅ Legal compliance
✅ Brand guidelines
✅ Customer testimonials

## Review Checklist

Before approving any generated content, verify:
- [ ] Tone matches platform and brand voice
- [ ] No prohibited content included
- [ ] Facts and claims are accurate
- [ ] Grammar and spelling are correct
- [ ] Hashtags are relevant and appropriate
- [ ] Call-to-action is clear
- [ ] Character limits respected
- [ ] No sensitive information disclosed
- [ ] Brand personality shines through
- [ ] Content provides value to audience
